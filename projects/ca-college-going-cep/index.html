California High School College-Going Rates & CEP Partner Analysis

This project introduces exploratory data analysis using education data. The analysis examines college-going rates across California high schools and compares CEP partner schools to other schools across the state.

The goal is to identify geographic patterns and better understand where CEP partnerships are located relative to college-going outcomes.

  • How do college-going rates vary across California counties and school districts?
  • What patterns do you notice about CEP partner schools compared to other schools?

A public repository for this project will be linked here once available.
