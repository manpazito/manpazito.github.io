Turbine Blade Multi-Objective Optimization

While taking ENGIN 26 - Three-Dimensional Modeling for Design at Berkeley, we needed a way to justify our turbine blade profile, so I decided to build a playful blade design explorer inspired by Li et al. (2023).

Instead of hunting for one "perfect" blade, I treated this like a design challenge where multiple strong options can exist, then compared them to see which ones felt like the best overall balance.
